      <h2>Hi, I'm Siddha 👋</h2>
      <p>I am a freelance software developer deeply invested in the mechanics of artificial intelligence. Rather than treating AI as a mysterious vending machine that solves everything, I spend an unreasonable amount of time digging into machine learning architectures, model training, and deep neural networks to understand what exactly is happening under the hood. Whether I am architecting SaaS products or developing full-stack web applications for clients, my goal is to write clean, efficient software while occasionally wrestling with my compiler until it finally agrees with me, and politely negotiating with my AI models so they stop hallucinating.</p>
      <p>Beyond the screen, I am heavily driven by technical leadership and bringing developers together. I spearheaded the inaugural SCICODE intercollegiate fest as head organizer, founded the BitCA v2.4 club, and drove the digital infrastructure for Fiestron 2024. I believe that building a strong ecosystem is just as important as building good software. I actively embed myself in the broader tech industry. You can usually find me at major industry meetups and tech summits, getting completely mesmerized by the wild inner workings of Web3 (and trying my hardest not to fall down another decentralized rabbit hole), all while using these spaces as prime territory to expand my network, document the tech journey, and collaborate with fellow builders.</p>
      <p>When I am not debugging or organizing tech events, I treat the learning process like a competitive sport. I am currently expanding my creative toolkit by diving into 3D modeling with Blender, After Effects, and video editing. My ultimate endgame is to merge these visual disciplines with my programming background to venture into game development, because why spend hours playing mobile battle royale games when you can eventually just build your own?</p>
